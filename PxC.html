What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique was created by Francesco Cirillo to help with one's productivity and learning. This time management technique breaks down one's time into intervals to promote sustained concentration and prevent mental fatigue. The intervals are traditionally broken down to 25 minutes for work and 5 minutes for a break. The cycle continues until one finishes their tasks.
